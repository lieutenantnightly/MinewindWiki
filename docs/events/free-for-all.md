# Team Deathmatch

Free For All is a [[events|event]] that takes place in spawn. Players will fight one another in order to obtain points and retain their ranking on leaderboard.

- Time Span: 15 Minutes.
- Takes place in spawn. 
- Players can kill others for 1 point.
- Dying with 4+ points will award the slayer 25% of your total score if they have fewer points than you.
- If players leave spawn voluntarily they will lose their score

| Rank |	Prizes |
| --- | --- |
| Top 15 | 1x Jester Key<br/>1 20 Dragon Eggs |
| Top 16+ | 20 Dragon Eggs |
