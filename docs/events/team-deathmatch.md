# Team Deathmatch

Team Deathmatch is a co-op [[events|event]] that takes place in spawn. Players will be catagorized into two seperate teams at random; Red and Aqua. Each team will fight the oppossing team over the span of 15 minutes until the timer reaches zero; in which the team with the most overall kills will be declared the winner of the event.

- Time Span: 15 Minutes.
- Takes place in spawn.
- Each team has their own point leaderboard.
- Players can kill others for 1 point.
- Dying with 4+ points will award the slayer 25% of your total score if they have fewer points than you.
- Players can leave spawn voluntarily and keep score.

| Rank |	Prizes |
| --- | --- |
| Winning Team | 1 Jester Key<br>/1 32 Dragon Eggs|
| Losing Team | 32 Dragon Eggs |
