# Bait

Fishing Event is an [[events|event]] often referred to as Bait.

Time Span: 15 Minutes

- Takes place in spawn
- The most items caught wins top prize
- Players can kill others and steal their total score
- Players that are killed are sent back to their spawn and lose their score
- If players leave spawn voluntarily they will lose their score
